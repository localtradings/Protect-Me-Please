# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but address risk clearly.
- Keep discussion focused on improving the project.
- Respect security boundaries and responsible disclosure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information
- Encouraging unauthorized testing or abuse
- Sharing weaponized payloads outside the project safety model

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or ban participants who violate this code of conduct.
